# Acme Robotics Employee Handbook

Welcome to Acme Robotics. This handbook summarizes the policies every employee
should know. It is a generic sample used to exercise the RAG system.

## Working Hours and Leave

Standard working hours are 9:00 to 17:00, Monday to Friday. Employees accrue
20 days of paid annual leave per year, plus public holidays. Leave requests must
be submitted at least two weeks in advance through the HR portal.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to three days per week with manager approval.
Remote workers must remain reachable during core hours of 10:00 to 15:00 and
are responsible for securing any company data on their devices.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses are reimbursed within 30 days of submitting a claim. Receipts
are required for any expense over 25 dollars. Travel must be booked through the
approved corporate travel agent to qualify for reimbursement.

## Information Security

All laptops must use full-disk encryption and a screen lock. Passwords must be
at least 14 characters. Report any suspected security incident to the security
team within 24 hours. Customer data must never be copied to personal accounts.
